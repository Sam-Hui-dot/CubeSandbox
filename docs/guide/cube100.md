# Cube 100 Program

**Cube 100 · Founding Users Program** — A case study initiative for the first 100 teams running AI agents in production.

## Overview

The Cube 100 Program is a global call for the first **100 real, tellable, and measurable** AI agent sandbox case studies — from teams and organizations already using, or about to use, CubeSandbox.

Since going open source in April 2026, CubeSandbox has surpassed 10,000 GitHub stars in 80 days, shipped 11 releases, welcomed nearly 70 global contributors, and accumulated over 560 commits. Teams are already running Cube in production — powering AI agents, coding copilots, and data-analysis workloads.

The Cube 100 Program aims to systematically capture these real-world practices, giving the community reference architectures and deployment blueprints to learn from — while offering selected teams full-stack engineering and ecosystem support.

## What to Include in Your Case Study

Your submission should cover the following:

| Section | What to include |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Adoption stage** | Where you are with CubeSandbox today (production / POC / personal project, etc.) |
| **Use case** | The role CubeSandbox plays in your stack (e.g. agent code-execution sandbox, data-analysis environment, CI/testing sandbox, agent-platform foundation) |
| **Core challenge** | The key technical or business problem you faced before choosing Cube |
| **Solution** | How you solved it with CubeSandbox — including your architecture and the features you relied on |
| **Measurable outcomes** | At least one externally shareable metric (e.g. cold-start latency, sandbox density, infrastructure cost, throughput) |
| **Deployment scale** | Current Cube deployment size (internal testing / small-scale production / medium-to-large production) |
| **Migration context** *(optional)* | What you were using before and why you switched to Cube |

We recommend attaching supporting materials such as architecture diagrams, deployment screenshots, or key-metric charts.

## Selection Rules

- The first cohort is capped at **100 seats**. First come, first reviewed, first accepted.
- Applications not selected in this round automatically roll into the next candidate pool with priority review.

**Selection criteria:**

| Criterion | What we look for |
|-----------|------------------|
| Authenticity | A real business scenario — not a pure demo. You can share at least one of: architecture diagram, deployment screenshot, or a key metric. |
| Narrative clarity | A clear use case, a thoughtful stack choice, and a solid answer to "why Cube?" |
| Measurable impact | At least one publicly shareable metric — performance, cost, efficiency, or scale. Any one works. |
